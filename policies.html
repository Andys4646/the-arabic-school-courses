Policies & Terms

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

These policies explain how enrolment, payments, cancellations, refunds, class conduct, and student data are managed at The Arabic School.

Privacy Enrolment Cancellation Refund Conduct General Terms

Privacy Policy

  • We collect only the data needed for admissions and class operations: name, email, payment status, and selected courses.
  • Your information is used for enrolment, scheduling, communication, and student support.
  • We do not sell personal data or share it for advertising.
  • You may request data correction or deletion through official school contact channels.

Enrolment & Payment Policy

  • Your seat is confirmed when registration is accepted and required payment is completed.
  • Published course fees apply at enrolment unless a written discount or scholarship is granted.
  • Payment plans, when offered, must be completed on schedule to maintain active enrolment.
  • The school may place enrolment on hold for overdue payments.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing.
  • If cancelled before the first session, students may request a refund under the refund policy or request a one-time transfer to a future cohort.
  • Missed classes after course start are treated as absences, not cancellations.
  • If the school cancels or postpones a course, students are offered a transfer option or eligible refund.

Refund Policy

  • 100% refund if The Arabic School cancels a course before launch.
  • 100% refund if requested within 7 days of payment and at least 7 days before the first live class.
  • 50% refund if requested after that window but before the second live class starts.
  • No refund from the date of the second live class onward.
  • Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 10 business days.

Code of Conduct

  • Students must communicate respectfully with teachers and peers.
  • Harassment, discriminatory language, repeated disruption, and academic misconduct are not permitted.
  • The school may remove participants who harm the learning environment.
  • Removal due to serious misconduct may occur without refund.

General Policies & Terms

  • Classes are delivered online at scheduled times shared after enrolment confirmation.
  • Course materials are for personal educational use only and may not be resold, republished, or redistributed.
  • Recordings, when provided, are for enrolled students only and may not be publicly shared.
  • The school may update curricula, instructors, and schedules while preserving academic standards.

For policy questions, contact: info@the-arabic-school-courses.com
